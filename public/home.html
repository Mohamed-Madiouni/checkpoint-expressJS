The Mission

Increase access to high-quality education for everyone, everywhere Enhance teaching and learning on campus and online Advance teaching and learning through research

Our Story

MLS is the trusted platform for education and learning. MLS is home to more than 20 million learners, the majority of top-ranked universities in the world and industry-leading companies. As a global nonprofit, MLS is transforming traditional education, removing the barriers of cost, location and access. Fulfilling the demand for people to learn on their own terms, MLS is reimagining the possibilities of education, providing the highest-quality, stackable learning experiences.
